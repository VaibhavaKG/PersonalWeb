Projects & Research

Featured Project

From Stardust to Stars: Stellar Evolution and Dynamics

An analytical study on stellar life cycles — detailing the physics of molecular cloud collapse, main-sequence nuclear fusion, and the final stages of stellar collapse. Focuses on the Hertzsprung-Russell diagram, mass-luminosity scaling relations, and compact stellar endpoints (white dwarfs, neutron stars, and black holes), utilizing observational datasets from NASA, Hubble, and the Event Horizon Telescope.

Geospatial Tech & Remote Sensing

Completed modules with IIRS/ISRO on satellite-based urban growth mapping, thermal environments, land-use classification, and GIS spatial modeling.

Semiconductor Tech & Microfabrication

Participated in IISc CeNSE programs exploring photolithography, ALD/CVD deposition rates, and MOS device characterization.

Web Development & Computational Tools

Founding member of the Coding Club at IISER Thiruvananthapuram; developed the official Science & Technology Council web platform.

Science Communication & Speculative Fiction

Analyzing how speculative science in literature translates complex physical theories into public understanding, bridging humanities and physics.

About

I am a physics major at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Thiruvananthapuram, working at the intersection of theoretical physics, stellar dynamics, and computational astronomy. My academic journey is driven by a curiosity about the mechanisms that govern extreme environments in the cosmos.

Beyond core physics, I am interested in coding and software development, organizing events as a founding member of the Coding Club and developing web tools for the Science & Tech Council. I also appreciate literature and science writing, exploring how narrative structures can help communicate complex concepts.

Based in Mysuru, Karnataka, and studying in Thiruvananthapuram. Always open to conversations about astrophysics, programming, or the space between.

Currently focused on undergraduate coursework in quantum mechanics, statistical physics, and data analysis, while compiling observational evidence for stellar structure models.
